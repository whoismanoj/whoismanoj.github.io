My name is Manoj Shakya. I am a third year Ph.D. student at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore. I am under the supervison of Prof. Francis Lee (NTU) and Dr. Huey Yuen (SIMTech, A\*star). My area of research is Deep Reinforcement Learning. The application side of my research focuses on the supply chain optimization techniques using deep reinforcement learning.

Before I joined NTU, I was an Assistant Professor at <a href="https://comp.ku.edu.np/">Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Kathmandu University</a>. Currently, I am on study leave. I graduated with a Master of Engineering in Computer Science and Technology from <a href='http://www-en.hnu.edu.cn/'>Hunan University</a>, China and Bachelor of Computer Science and Engineering from <a href='https://ku.edu.np'>Kathmandu University</a>, Nepal

Besides research and teaching, I love watching movies, documentaries about nature/space/war and biographies. I love traveling around the world too.
