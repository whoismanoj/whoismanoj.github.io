My name is Manoj Shakya. I am a final year Ph.D. student at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore. I am under the supervison of <a href="https://scholar.google.com.sg/citations?user=TyytTfQAAAAJ&hl=en">A/Prof. Francis Lee Bu-Sung (NTU)</a> and <a href='https://www.linkedin.com/in/hueyyuen/?originalSubdomain=sg'>Dr. Ng Huey Yuen (SIMTech, A\*STAR)</a>. My area of research is Deep Reinforcement Learning. The application side of my research focuses on the supply chain optimization techniques.

Before I enrolled into NTU, I was an Assistant Professor at <a href="https://comp.ku.edu.np/">Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Kathmandu University</a>. Currently, I am on study leave. I graduated with a Master of Engineering in Computer Science and Technology from <a href='http://www-en.hnu.edu.cn/'>Hunan University, China</a> and Bachelor of Computer Science and Engineering from <a href='https://ku.edu.np'>Kathmandu University, Nepal</a>

Besides research and teaching, I like to watch movies, documentaries about nature/space/war and biographies of big personalities. I love traveling around the world too.
